Our Story

A small cafe that grew on word of mouth — and a 4.5★ rating.

We never set out to be fancy. Just consistently good — the kind of place where the chai is always hot, the staff remembers your order, and there's always a seat by the window.

01

Fresh, Every Single Day

Nothing sits in the back. Sandwiches are pressed and shakes are blended only after you order.

02

A Neighbourhood Favourite

Loved enough by Palam locals to earn one of the highest cafe ratings in the area — without ever running an ad.

03

Open Late, Open Always

From your first coffee of the day to a late-night snack run, we're rarely closed when you need us.
